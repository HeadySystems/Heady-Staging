## Portfolio Strategy

**Single story, multiple entry points:**

1. **Start**: IDE/Companion (low friction)
2. **Expand**: MCP connectors (stickiness)
3. **Standardize**: Platform/Cloud governance
4. **Retain**: Monitoring + audit + reproducibility
